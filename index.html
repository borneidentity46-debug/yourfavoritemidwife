Natural Methods of Conceiving

Overview: Natural fertility coaching focuses on optimizing the couple's body and environment for conception without medical procedures.

Key elements

  • Cycle awareness: BBT, cervical mucus tracking, and LH testing to find fertile windows.
  • Nutrition & supplements: Evidence-backed guidance on diet, micronutrients, and individualized supplementation.
  • Stress & sleep: Interventions for cortisol reduction and sleep quality to support hormones.
  • Bodywork & movement: Gentle pelvic care, physiotherapy referrals, and tailored exercise.

Medical Guidance & Diagnostics

Overview: Medical guidance involves working with licensed providers to diagnose and treat underlying causes of infertility.

Typical steps

  • History & labs: AMH, FSH, TSH, prolactin, semen analysis.
  • Imaging: pelvic ultrasound & HSG for tubal patency.
  • Referral navigation: prepare questions and documentation for specialists.
  • Medication education: ovulation induction meds and protocols.

Assisted Reproduction (IUI / IVF / Donor)

Overview: Assisted reproduction includes IUI, IVF, egg/sperm donation, and gestational carrier options.

  • Preparation: medication schedules, trigger shots, and clinic instructions.
  • Emotional support: counseling and coping strategies through cycles.
  • Logistics: clinic referrals, cost guidance, and donor coordination.
  • Peri-procedure care: nutrition, rest plans, and early monitoring.
